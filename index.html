About Me

Brandon is a highly skilled and creative software engineer and project manager. He has a wealth of experience in both hands-on development and in project management of a wide variety of applications and IT solutions for internal and public stakeholders. With a diverse background in public health, science, and government; Brandon brings to the table unique executive, regulatory, and geographic information systems (GIS) skills and experience to IT projects and software.

Brandon's love for technology is seen through creating, experimenting, and manging new ideas into reality. He has a passion for helping and empowering others through technology, especially in the ethos of bitcoin, personal, and financial self-soveringty. He believes in the importance of FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) and you'll often find him contributing on numerous projects and collaborating with the community with the same energy and enthusiasm.

When he's not lost in the world of development (or bitcoin 😜), you'll find Brandon indulging in his love for great food, spending time with friends and family, or tuning into his favorite podcast or book.
